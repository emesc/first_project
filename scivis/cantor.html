Cantor set: The unchanging growth



The Cantor Set was the preliminary version of a fractal. It copies itself, shrinking each duplicate by a factor of 3 and then translated. This is where its self-similarity feature comes in.

A few number of steps are needed to generate this design.

  1. begin with a horizontal line segment
  2. copy the line and translate
  3. divide the line from (2) into three parts
  4. remove the center part leaving you with two smaller lines with space in the middle
  5. repeat (1) to (4)
