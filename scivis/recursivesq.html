Recursive Squares



The Sierpinski carpet is a Cantor set rendered on 2 dimensions.


I want to see how it would look like if the slowly shrinking copies of itself appear only on the corners.

Let me begin with a single square.

Then make copies of itself, half the size of the original, with their centers on each corner of the first square.

And keep on doing this until the size of tiniest square is too small to be pleasing to my eyes.


Hmm.. Sierpinski carpet still looks better.
