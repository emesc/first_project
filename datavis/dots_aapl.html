An interactive scatterplot

one dependent and one independent variable



What follows is a simple scatterplot marking the opening and closing prices of Apple Inc extracted from Yahoo Finance. Using the csv link at the bottom of that page, let's cover the dates of 2014's first quarter (02 Jan to 21 Mar). All prices are in USD.

The first chart right below include only a run from 0 to the maximum closing price. Unlike the line graph, instead of using grids as a helper in identifying values, I will include a bit of interaction.

This graph enables interaction. Simply place your mouse cursor over the dots to see the dates and the corresponding adjusted closing prices.
