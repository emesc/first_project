Sexual satisfaction per capita GDP

3 variables in one graph



There are a lot of datasets freely available online. I happened to chance upon this list. I didn't bother to verify its reliability anymore; it's an interesting source though for another scatterplot.

As indicated by the title, the following is a graph of the sexual satisfaction vs per capita GDP and the percentage of the population of selected countries. The size of the dots follows the rate of satisfaction. This means I have mapped the minimum and maximum rate to 1 and 20 pixels, respectively. Everything in between are converted accordingly. The axis labels this time are shifted within the chart, standing by their axis. The table is provided right after the chart where I have taken the liberty to sort the satisfaction rate in descending fashion.

Once more, this is an interactive graph. Try hovering your mouse cursor on the dots to see more details.
